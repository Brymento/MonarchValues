MonarchValues

What is MonarchValues?

MonarchValues is a political compass test, made in cooperation with the "Kingdom of Based", that combines its own original questions with the UI of 8values to calculate what type of Monarchist are you, or if you even are one. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a political compass to reflect your monarchist values.

* A few questions have been reworded as I feel some of them were poorly written.

There are questions in the test.

Why use this version?

Because there really weren't any other test where the main focus was monarchy, there were some that include it as an axis or a result, but none were really made with monarchism as its main focus, until now!
